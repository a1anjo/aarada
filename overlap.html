Introduction

Delta Overlap, an Italian company, is a leading manufacturer in the residential garage door market. Renowned for its innovative "Overlap" concept, Delta Overlap offers a unique solution that eliminates ceiling tracks, maximizing garage space. The operator is integrated into the door frame's upper beam, allowing two sections to move seamlessly with a patented locking system. Delta Overlap combines advanced technology with skilled craftsmanship.

Sectional Doors

The Overlap sectional door has insulated panels, anti-pinch system, zinc-plated tracks, and torsion springs.

  • Anti-pinch and anti-crushing "finger-safe" system
  • Electro-zinc-plated steel components with anti-fall device and torsion spring balancing
  • Panels in 500 and 610 by 40 mm thickness with embossed Sendzimir galvanized steel

Overlap Doors

The Overlap door innovatively opens without ceiling guides, halving space, with a motor in the top frame.

  • No ceiling guides needed, freeing up ceiling space
  • Motor integrated into the upper frame crosspiece
  • Reduces internal space usage by half

Overbased Doors

Sleek overbased doors for garages & industrial use with retractable engine, counterweight mechanics, & anti-burglary features for smooth, secure operation.

  • Retractable engine eliminates need for ceiling guides
  • Counterweight mechanics ensure reliability
  • Trackless design for smooth, obstruction-free operation

Entrance Doors

Delta Overlap entrance doors offer certified performance: RC 5 burglary resistance, thermal transmittance, and acoustic performance for excellence.

  • RC 5 burglary resistance
  • Thermal transmittance: 1.00 W/m²K
  • Acoustic performance: 38 dB

Bioclimatic Pergola

Delta Overlap bioclimatic pergola offers adjustable sunlight, natural ventilation, and rain protection with aluminum slats rotating up to 140°. Control via winch, sensors, or radio.
