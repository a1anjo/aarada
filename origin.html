Introduction

Origin Global is a British company specializing in the design and manufacture of high-quality aluminum doors and windows.

Origin Global's product range includes bi-fold doors, sliding doors, and residential doors, as well as windows and various accessories. The company's products are known for their durability, energy efficiency, and aesthetic appeal. They are often customizable, allowing for a range of configurations and finishes to meet customer specifications.

The company operates primarily in the UK but has expanded its market reach internationally, including the United States and other countries. Origin Global prides itself on its rigorous quality control processes and commitment to sustainable practices, utilizing recyclable materials and implementing energy-efficient manufacturing processes.
